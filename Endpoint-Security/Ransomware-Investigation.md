# Ransomware Investigation

## Objective

Investigate ransomware activity and determine scope of impact.

## Data Sources

* CrowdStrike Falcon
* Defender XDR
* Sentinel

## Investigation Steps

### Step 1

Identify affected systems.

### Step 2

Review file encryption activity.

### Step 3

Identify initial infection vector.

### Step 4

Review lateral movement.

### Step 5

Determine affected users and assets.

## Containment

* Isolate systems
* Disable compromised accounts
* Block malicious indicators
* Begin recovery process

## MITRE ATT&CK

* T1486 Data Encrypted for Impact
